What is The Cabin Library?

The Cabin Library is an online magazine website featuring books.

It was created on the 12th of November, year 2021, by Jashleen Kate Medina, for her 9th Grade Computer Science subject.

Here, you can find books from different genres like Fantasy, Sci-Fi, and Horror!
We strive to provide you with books to read and new adventures to experience.
If you crave reading a book while sitting by the fireplace in a cabin and sipping hot chocolate, then The Cabin Library is for you!
